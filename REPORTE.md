                                        NATIONAL LEVEL ANALYSIS OF THE TELECOMMUNICATIONS SECTOR
                                                          INTERNET SERVICE

This analysis focuses on fixed internet access in Argentina, highlighting the differences between provinces.

It is evident that the telecommunications sector plays a leading role in societies and economies around the world. Virtually all economic sectors, as well as personal communications of the population, depend on Cellular Telephony and Internet as the main means.

This analysis focuses on internet service, based on information available from different datasets (source: https://api.datosabiertos.enacom.gob.ar/api/v2/datasets/), after being visualized through the Dashboard (PI_03/HenryPI_03.twbx).

The data mostly corresponds to the period 2014 - 2022 (until 1st quarter). They are sufficient to observe the trends of different indicators. When referring to specific data, they will be approximated for convenience.

Total accesses (in thousands of pesos!) for fixed internet provision (all providers): An exponential growth is observed, from 2.9 million in 2014-1Q to 51.4M in 2022-1Q. Accesses per 100 households, at the national level, show quasi-linear growth, growing at a slower rate than accesses for the same period. The average download speed follows the total accesses in its growth, taking an exponential form from 2017.

Distribution of the number of accesses per 100 households in the territory: For 2014, there is a high concentration of accesses in the capital federal and Tierra del Fuego, with large differences between provinces, which do not directly correlate with the population of each one. In more recent periods, more even distributions are observed, although there are STILL LARGE differences between provinces. This characteristic may be due to various factors such as the amount of rural population, economic level, and culture. Surely we will see in the future how this measure evens out for the entire geography, thanks to the proliferation of different types of technologies (3G, 4G, satellite).

Broadband vs Dial-up connections, by province: Although broadband connections outnumber dial-up connections in all provinces, broadband has not grown as expected, with Buenos Aires showing the highest increase and corresponding decrease in dial-up. Followed by CABA, Cordoba, and Santa Fe, which show moderate growth. The rest of the provinces have almost flat behaviors but are increasing. Should we expect and work for this behavior to be unified across the entire territory? Is this due to the vast and sparsely populated territory of the country? Should we aim for wireless-satellite technologies for remote urbanizations? To what extent is this feasible? How to prevent foreign companies from dominating the market through investments and innovative technologies? These questions go beyond the scope of this analysis but deserve much attention in the near future. What is evident is that high-speed internet will be available to all citizens in the near future.

It is relevant, then, to analyze the current types of technologies and their growth (and decline!) to direct investments in the right direction. (Complement with analysis of emerging technologies worldwide). For this, we observe the table 'ACCESOS POR TIPO DE TECNOLOGIA' (Accesses by type of technology) for each province and national average, for the period 2014-2022. We compare ADSL, Cable Modem, Fiber Optics, and Wireless. I will leave out the 'Others' category as its composition is unknown.

Nationwide: On the rise:
-Cablemodem, more than doubling its total share.
-Wireless, more than tripling its share, but still not having a significant presence (<1/10 of Cablemodem).
-Optical Fiber, showing exponential growth, going from a minimal share in 2014 to almost 1/4 of the total in 2022!
On the decline:
-ADSL, the net decrease in quantity is approximately half, but thanks to the significant growth of other technologies, its total share goes from being more than half in 2014 to approximately 1/7 in 2022!

Provincial level: A special case for San Luis, where the majority of the population uses Wireless connection, as the province provides it for free (?) starting from the 3rd quarter of 2018. It does not reflect the general trend.

Due to the significant population disparity between provinces, it is clear which ones are more important (see 'POPULATION BY PROVINCES' table). Buenos Aires, CABA, Cordoba, and Santa Fe concentrate the MAJORITY of the population.

-Buenos Aires, shows very similar behavior to the national trend!
-CABA, absolute predominance of Cablemodem (and it shows the highest increase); very little Optical Fiber (need to further investigate the reason for this) but still more than doubling in quantity; significant decrease in ADSL.
-Cordoba, similar to the national trend in composition and behavior, but with a higher growth of Wireless and Optical Fiber (almost 1/4 of the total)!
-Santa Fe, also similar to the national trend, with significant growth of Optical Fiber, but not reaching 1/4 of the share; predominance of Cablemodem, with growth but at a slower pace than Optical Fiber.

Next group of provinces (with population between 1M and 1.6M): Mendoza, Entre Rios, Tucuman, Salta.

-Mendoza, significant increase in Optical Fiber, approximately 1/2 of the total share, surpassing Cablemodem!
-Entre Rios, predominance of Cablemodem (approximately 1/2 of the share), very little Optical Fiber compared to Wireless. Decrease in ADSL.
-Tucuman, even growth of Optical Fiber (surpassing Cablemodem with over 1/3 of the share).
-Salta, predominance of Cablemodem with high growth; minimal growth of Optical Fiber, but with less than 1/4 of the total share in the last period.

Having observed the trends, the national trend is confirmed for the most relevant cases and even for provinces with low population. With exceptions. As a general trend, the emergence of Optical Fiber as a potential market leader and Cablemodem with absolute predominance and widespread growth are highlighted.

An analysis of international trends and emerging technologies, as well as other players in the Telecommunications industry, is left for future analysis.

Este analisis se focaliza en el acceso a internet fijo en Argentina, haciendo notar las diferencias entre provincias.

Es evidente que el sector telecomunicaciones juega un rol protagonista en las sociedades y economias de todo el mundo. Practicamente todos los sectores a nivel economico, asi como las comunicaciones personales de la poblacion, dependen de la Telefonia Celular e Internet como los principales medios. 

Este analisis se concentra en el servicio de internet. Desprendiendose de la informacion disponible en diferentes Datasets (fuente https://api.datosabiertos.enacom.gob.ar/api/v2/datasets/), luego de ser visualizada mediante el Dashboard (PI_03/HenryPI_03.twbx).

Los datos corresponden en su mayoria al periodo 2014 - 2022 (hasta 1er trimestre). Son suficientes para observar las tendencias de diferentes marcadores. Cuando se haga referencia a datos puntuales, seran aproximados por conveniencia.

Accesos totales (en miles de pesos!), para la provision de internet fijo (todos los prestadores):
Se observa un crecimiento exponencial, pasando de 2,9 millones en 2014-1T, a  51,4M en 2022-1T. 
Accesos cada 100 hogares, a nivel nacional, presenta un creciemiento cuasi lineal, creciendo a un ritmo menor que los accesos para el mismo periodo. 
La velocidad media de bajada, acompania a los accesos totales en su crecimiento, tomando forma exponencial a partir de 2017. 

Distribucion de la cantidad de accesos cada 100 hogares en el territorio:
Para 2014 se observa gran concentracion de accesos en capital federal, y Tierra del Fuego, con grandes diferencias entre provincias,  lo cual no correlaciona directamente con la poblacion de cada una. En los periodos mas recientes, se observan ditribuciones mas parejas, igualmente habiendo GRANDES diferencias entre provincias. Esta caracteristica puede deberse a distintos factores como cantidad de poblacion rural, nivel economico y cultural. Seguramente veremos en el futuro como esta medida se empareja para toda la geografia, gracias a la proliferacion de diferentes tipos de tecnologias (3G, 4G, satelital).

Seguido por CABA, Cordoba y Santa fe, que presentan crecimento moderado. El resto de provincias tiene comportamientos casi 'chatos', pero en aumento.
